# SOC Metrics and Objectives

## **Core Metrics Overview**

**Purpose:**

SOC metrics measure how effectively a Security Operations Center protects digital assets by evaluating alert handling and detection accuracy.

| **Metric** | **Formula** | **Measures** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Alerts Count (AC)** | Total Count of Alerts Received | Analyst workload and alert volume |
| **False Positive Rate (FPR)** | False Positives / Total Alerts | Noise level and alert quality |
| **Alert Escalation Rate (AER)** | Escalated Alerts / Total Alerts | L1 analyst experience and independence |
| **Threat Detection Rate (TDR)** | Detected Threats / Total Threats | SOC’s detection reliability |

### **Key Insights per Metric**

- **Alerts Count**
    - Too many → Overload and missed threats.
    - Too few → Possible visibility issues or SIEM failure.
    - *Optimal:* 5–30 alerts/day per L1 analyst.
- **False Positive Rate (FPR)**
    - High noise reduces vigilance.
    - *Ideal:* Below 80%; 0% is unrealistic.
    - *Fix:* Tune detection rules and automate triage.
- **Alert Escalation Rate (AER)**
    - High rate → Overdependence on L2 analysts.
    - *Target:* <50%, ideally <20%.
    - Reflects analyst confidence and accuracy.
- **Threat Detection Rate (TDR)**
    - *Goal:* 100% detection — every missed threat risks major breaches.
    - Example: 4/6 detected → 67% TDR (poor).

## **Triage & SLA Metrics**

These metrics ensure **timely detection and response** to threats as defined in the **SOC Service Level Agreement (SLA).**

| **Metric** | **Common SLA** | **Description** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **SOC Team Availability** | 24/7 | Working schedule; can vary (e.g., 8/5 or 24/7). |
| **Mean Time to Detect (MTTD)** | 5 minutes | Time from attack start to detection by SOC tools. |
| **Mean Time to Acknowledge (MTTA)** | 10 minutes | Time for L1 to begin triage after alert generation. |
| **Mean Time to Respond (MTTR)** | 60 minutes | Time to contain or remediate the breach. |

**Timeline:**

Attack occurs → Detected (MTTD) → Acknowledged (MTTA) → Response executed (MTTR)

## **Improving SOC Metrics**

| **Issue** | **Recommended Actions** |
| --- | --- |
| **FPR > 80%** | - Exclude trusted activities (e.g., system updates).- Automate common alert triage (SOAR/scripts). |
| **MTTD > 30 min** | - Optimize detection rule frequency.- Ensure real-time log collection. |
| **MTTA > 30 min** | - Enable real-time notifications for alerts.- Balance alert queues among analysts. |
| **MTTR > 4 hours** | - Escalate threats rapidly to L2.- Maintain playbooks for attack scenarios. |

## **Summary Takeaways**

- **Core metrics** evaluate detection accuracy and analyst performance.
- **SLA metrics** focus on response timeliness and operational readiness.
- **Goal:** Continuous improvement in detection quality, speed, and efficiency — directly influencing SOC effectiveness and analyst career progression.
